Our services

Electronics manufacturing services, end to end.

Five concrete services delivered from our assembly unit in Ahmedabad — focused on quality components, dependable assembly, and small to mid-volume production.

EMS — Electronics Manufacturing Services

We provide full electronics manufacturing services that span component procurement, board assembly, and functional testing. You bring the design and bill of materials; we handle the build process and quality checks. This is suited to OEMs and product companies that want a single partner across the production cycle.

OEM Assembly

We carry out build-to-print assembly of electronic products to your exact specifications and BOM. Work is performed against documented instructions with consistent in-process checks, so finished units match what your design intends. We focus on repeatable quality rather than oversized capacity claims.

PCB Manufacturing

We handle SMD (surface-mount) and through-hole PCB assembly for small to mid-volume production runs. Boards are assembled with attention to solder quality and inspected to catch defects early. This service fits prototypes, pilot batches, and ongoing low-to-mid-volume builds.

Contract Manufacturing

We manage outsourced production runs on your behalf, coordinating component sourcing, assembly, and delivery to an agreed schedule. This lets product teams hand off manufacturing while keeping visibility of the build. Scope and volumes are agreed up front so expectations stay realistic.

Component Sourcing

We source electronic components against your bill of materials, helping you secure the right parts at the quality your build requires. Our focus is on reliable procurement and suitable alternatives where a part is constrained. Sourcing can be offered on its own or as part of a wider assembly engagement.
