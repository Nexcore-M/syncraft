What we do

Five core electronics manufacturing services, delivered from our assembly unit in Ahmedabad.

EMS

End-to-end electronics manufacturing services covering procurement, assembly, and testing for OEM and product-company clients.

OEM Assembly

Build-to-print assembly of electronic products to your specifications and bill of materials, with consistent quality control.

PCB Manufacturing

SMD and through-hole assembly for small to mid-volume runs, with attention to solder quality and inspection.

Contract Manufacturing

Outsourced production runs managed on your behalf, coordinating sourcing, assembly, and delivery against your schedule.

Component Sourcing

Reliable procurement of electronic components, helping you secure the right parts for your build at the quality you need.
