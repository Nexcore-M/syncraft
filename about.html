About us

Established 2023, in Ahmedabad.

Syncraft Electronics is a newly established electronics manufacturing services partner. We operate an electronics assembly unit in Ahmedabad, Gujarat, and work with OEM and product-company clients on their builds.

As a young and growing company, our approach is focused and practical: quality components, reliable procurement, and dependable assembly. We'd rather earn trust through consistent work than make claims about scale or history we don't yet have.

What we focus on

Three principles guide how we work as we grow.

Quality components

We prioritise the right parts at the right quality, so the boards we assemble perform as your design intends.

Reliable procurement

We work to source components dependably against your BOM and to flag suitable alternatives when parts are constrained.

Dependable assembly

SMD and through-hole assembly carried out with in-process inspection, aimed at consistent, repeatable results.

Who we work with

We partner with OEMs and product companies that need electronics built to their design — from prototypes and pilot batches through small to mid-volume production. If you're bringing a hardware product to market and need a manufacturing partner who is invested in getting the details right, we'd like to hear from you.

A note on where we are today

Syncraft Electronics is genuinely new — founded in 2023 and still growing. We describe our capabilities plainly and don't claim certifications, client rosters, or capacity we haven't earned. What we offer is focused attention and honest communication on every build.
